Description

Aqua is the ultimate tool for tracking and improving your daily water intake. Whether you're an athlete looking to optimize your hydration levels, or just someone who wants to make sure they're getting enough water every day, Aqua has you covered. With an easy-to-use interface and customizable daily hydration goals, Aqua makes it simple to monitor your water intake and stay on track.

With a lot of features:

  • Set personalized daily hydration goals
  • Log your water intake with the tap of a button
  • View your daily, weekly, and monthly hydration history
  • Monitor your progress and see how close you are to reaching your goals
  • Get reminders to drink water throughout the day
